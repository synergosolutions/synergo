How to download Synergo for Google Chrome:

1. Download the extension source code from Dropbox.
2. In the top right corner there will be a "Download" dropdown. Click on it and select "Direct Download". Unzip the file and keep it ready.
3. Go to your extensions dashboard at chrome://extensions/, make sure "Developer Mode" is turned on in the top right corner.
4. Click on "Load Unpacked" on the top left corner and input the unzipped folder you downloaded on step 1.
5. Close the tab and open a new tab! You have downloaded sucessfully.
